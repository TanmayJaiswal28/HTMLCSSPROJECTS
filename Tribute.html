Tribute to Nikola Tesla

The man who lit the world

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system.

Achievements:

  • Invented the Tesla coil
  • Pioneered the development of AC electricity
  • Developed early radio technology
  • Contributed to wireless transmission of energy
  • Held over 300 patents worldwide

Legacy:

Tesla's visionary ideas and inventions paved the way for numerous modern technologies. Despite facing financial and personal hardships, he remains a symbol of innovation and creativity. Today, he is celebrated as one of the greatest inventors in history.
